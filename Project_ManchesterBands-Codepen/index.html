Manchester Bands

Manchester has produced the best musicians for genarations.

Here are some of Manchesters finest

  • 1991-2009-Oasis Formed in Manchester in 1991, the same year as Chesney Hawkes released 'one and only' thank god he was a one hit wonder. "Supersonic" was the debut single for the Manchester Band. Written by lead guitarist Noel Gallagher.Released on 11 April 1994 as Oasis' debut single and later appeared on their debut album Defintely Maybe in August 1994.
  • 1983-Stone Roses the stone Roses were the leaders of the "Madchester" scene. The leaders of British guitar culture from the late 80s and early 90s. Their debut album became an English sensation and other groups followed The Charlatans, Inspiral Carpets and Happy Mondays.
  • The Smiths 1982-1987
  • Take That Since 1990
  • The Chemical Broters Since 1989
